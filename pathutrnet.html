PathUTRNet

Prediction of signaling pathways and microRNA non-coding regions with deep learning

  • Learn More

Summary

PathUTRNet consists of two deep learning models, which are utilized in a sequential way to accomplish three interconnected tasks:

  • The first model's purpose is to identify the existence of a binding site between a miRNA and non-coding region.
  • The second model's purpose is dual. That is predict both the signal transduction pathway the concatenated sequence (miRNA + UTR) belongs to and the untraslated region itself
Both models combine both CNN & RNN layers together, since this architecture outperforms both of the paper's simpler counterparts. Read the paper for more information.
